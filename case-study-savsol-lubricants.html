Savsol Lubricants
  • - User Experience
  • - Interface Design
  • - Interaction Design
Savsol Lubricants owned by Savita Oil Technologies is a leader in the lubricant and engine oil segment in India. It's parent company, Savita is a publically listed company with revenues of over 1000 cr.
Timeline

3 Week

Team

2 People

The
Challenge
Savsol, a product leader since 2007, realized it needed a brand revamp to cater to the new and growing millennial audience. It came to us with a challenge of conveying the new and reformed identity of The Green Performer to its consumers.
Our
Approach
Our team came up with the unique style approach of using the combination of The Green Performer mascot with supportive illustrated elements to create the brand environment for the website and maintaining the same feel through out the whole experience. The new website is fulfilling the brand commitments, easy to navigate actions for the wide range of green performance products and communicating the brand story through the interface without forcing into upon the user.

It was a pleasure working with Paper Pencil Productions and their team, with their speed, efficiency and interest in ensuring that the music bot was executed well. They completed the project proactively and ensured that maintenance was carried off without a hitch as well.

- Abhishek Agrawal,
Marketing Manager, Savsol Lubricants.
